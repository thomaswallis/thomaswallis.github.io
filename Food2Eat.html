Food2Eat



platform :

iOS (swift)

started :

19/05/2020

completed :

Ongoing

So, I'm a very fussy eater. I have the diet of a 10 year old and I'm also very indecisive with what I want to eat. Should I eat lasagne today or spaghetti and meatballs and so on.

Living in 2020 life is full of options of what to eat and what to do so much so that it can become overwhelming. This paradox of choice is why I have set out to create this app. I want it to be able to store a list of ingredients and their quantities and then at the press of a button you can spin a wheel and get given either one or two options of things to eat.

This is the first time that I will be working with iOS and this is because I plan to use it on an iPad mini that I use for everything in my house from turning the lights on and off, controlling the music in the house so it only makes sense to develop an app for this device. Later though I plan on making a version that is good for iPhones.



The base application should be able to do the following:

Be able to have a randomly selected recipe from the ingredients you have.

Have a pre set list of main ingredients and spices where users can add or subtract quantites.

Be able to click a button "I am making this" and then have that remove the correct quanitites from your own.

A section for other food the user might like.


I will be treating this page like a DevLog of all the work I do on this project.
